Glentleman Jim

Age: 2 years

Gender: Male

Size: Small

Breed: Flame Point/Siamese Mix

Shelter: Homeless Pet Placement League

City: Houston

Bio:

They say I am “excessively” friendly; I've been called "half Siamese - half Lab puppy" because I will bop right up to you expecting to be loved. I will converse like most Siamese, but my favorite activities are lying in a sunspot and then coming up for a good pet session -- how to choose, how to choose? I was rescued in the heat of July near an apartment complex, where no one was taking care of me and I was struggling to survive. Believe it or not, I was thin! Since I have been in my foster home, I have been thriving so much that I heard the word “diet". I have a few issues with allergies but come on, who doesn't in Houston? I get along best with other adult cats but honestly I think there is enough of me to love so I could definitely be your one-and-only. My edob is 1/13.
