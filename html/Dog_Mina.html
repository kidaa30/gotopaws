Mina

Age: Senior

Gender: Female

Size: Large

Breed: Shepherd Mix

Shelter: Muttville Senior Dog Rescue

City:San Francisco

Bio:

Mina has traveled over land and sea from Iran to be a part of Muttville and we couldn’t be happier to have her! This gentle girl has been through a lot of transitions in her life recently, so she is still understandably a bit wary of her surroundings. But with some patience and a lot of love, we’re sure that Mina will come out of her shell to show her wiggly and goofy side! Here’s what Mina’s foster mom has to say about this special girl:

"We’ve gone on a couple neighborhood walks. She met some neighbor dogs and seems to be getting relaxed. She loves to sniff everything and is sleeping right now in the same spot all the dogs like to sleep in. She is eating good, still peeing/pooping outside.”"

We think Mina is between 8-10 years old, weighing about 50 lbs.
